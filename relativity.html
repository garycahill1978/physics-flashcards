What this shows

Inside the train, the light travels straight up and down between mirrors. Outside, the train moves sideways while the light is in flight, so the light follows a longer diagonal path.

c is constant
γ = 1 / √(1 − v²/c²)
outside time = γ × train time

Relativity geometry

(c·t)² = (v·t)² + (c·τ)²

c²t² − v²t² = c²τ²

t²(c² − v²) = c²τ²

t = τ / √(1 − v²/c²)
